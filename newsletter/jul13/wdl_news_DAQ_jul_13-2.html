PCI Express
High-Speed Digitizer


The PXIe-9852 2-CH 14-bit 200 MS/s High-Speed Digitizer from ADLINK delivers high-accuracy measurement results and high-dynamic performance for long-term data recording applications such as distributed temperature sensing, radar signal testing, and atmospheric science research.

PXIe-9852 Features:
• PXI Express specification Rev. 2.0 compliant
• 2 simultaneously sampled 200 MS/s input channels with 14-bit resolution
• Up to 800 MB/s data streaming, and onboard signal averaging technology
• Up to 90 MHz bandwidth for analog input
• Full auto-calibration

Standalone
DAQ Module

USB-2405 USB DAQ Modules from ADLINK feature 24-bit high-performance dynamic signal acquisition equipped with 4 analog input channels providing simultaneous-sampling at up to 128 kS/s per channel.

USB-2405 Features:
• High-Speed USB 2.0
• 4-CH simultaneous sampling analog input
• AC or DC input coupling, software selectable
• Analog or digital triggering
• Supports 2mA excitation output on each channel for IEPE sensor measurement
• Full auto-calibration
• Ready-to-use testing application (U-Test) provided

Universal PCI
DAQ Solutions

PCI-DA12 Universal PCI Analog Output Cards from ACCES I/O feature 2, 4, 6, 8 and 16 (respectively), 12-bit, double-buffered, digital-to-analog converters. The PCI bus is a broadly available, popular option for industrial/rack-mount DAQ systems.

PCI-DA12 Features:
• Universal PCI, PCI-X, 3.3V and 5V compatible
• 2, 4, 6, 8 and 16, 12-bit, digital-to-analog converters
• Individual or simultaneous program control updates
• 24 or 16-bit digital I/O buffered with tri-stateable transceivers
• 3x 16-bit Down counters (models PCI-DA12-8/16 only)
• Resettable-fused +5VDC available
