JUMPtec Names WDL Systems Distributor of the Year

Pittsboro, NC – 15 July 2002 –JUMPtec (now known as Kontron) has named WDL Systems Distributor of the Year from among its 30 worldwide distributors. The announcement was made on June 24 in Deggendorf, Germany, during JUMPtec's annual distributors’ meeting. EMJ was cited for achieving top sales volume in 2001.

EMJ is a stocking distributor for JUMPtec products. Its website www.emjembedded.com provides spec sheets and online ordering and technical support. Sales and technical support are also available by calling EMJ toll-free. And EMJ promotes JUMPtec products through its active participation in embedded industry trade shows and advertising.

EMJ Vice President Mark Estill was present to accept the Distributor of the Year Award and said, "I am very pleased to accept this recognition on behalf of WDL Systems for the second year in a row. JUMPtec/Adastra (JUMPtec's America subsidiary) has been an excellent partner, and we would not be receiving this award without their support. JUMPtec's products are cutting-edge, and their features and flexibility put them in high demand in the American market."

JUMPtec Industrielle Computertechnik AG (Deggendorf) and Kontron Embedded Computers AG (Eching, near Munich) have merged since this Distributor of the Year award was given to WDL Systems. Under the terms of the merger, JUMPtec Adastra, the North American subsidiary of JUMPtec AG, will go by the name Kontron America.

 For additional information on Kontron, please visit: www.kontron.com

ABOUT KONTRON TECHNOLOGY
Kontron is a leading global embedded computer technology company. Kontron supplies a diversified customer base of OEMs, system integrators and application providers in the communications, automation, transportation, medical, military, aerospace, and test and measurement markets. The company helps its customers to considerably reduce their time-to-market and to gain a competitive advantage with products including high-performance open computer platforms and systems, single-board computers, man-machine interfaces and mobile computers. Kontron employs over 1100 people worldwide and has manufacturing facilities in Europe, North America and Asia Pacific. The company is listed on the German TecDAX 30 stock exchange under the symbol "KBC".

For additional information on Kontron, please visit: www.kontron.com


ABOUT WDL Systems
WDL Systems, a division of EMJ America Inc., distributes a full line of single board computers, CompactPCI solutions, Flash solutions, PC Card disk drives, PC Card readers, PC/104 add-on cards, PC/104 peripherals, displays, software and enclosures. EMJ is a publicly traded company with revenues of $150 million and can be found on the Toronto Stock Exchange under the symbol EMJ.
