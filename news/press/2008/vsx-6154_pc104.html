WDL SYSTEMS DISTRIBUTING THE VSX-6154 PC/104 SBC FROM ICOP TECHNOLOGY

Pittsboro, NC – 14 February 2008 – WDL Systems is proud to distribute the VSX-6154 PC/104 Single Board Computer from ICOP Technology. Designed for industrial and embedded applications, the VSX-6154 PC/104 SBC is based on a Vortex86SX System-On-Chip. Operating at 300MHz, this low-power SBC’s fanless design is suitable for broad range of uses such as data acquisition, industrial automation, process control, HMI, robotics, machinery control, and more.

“We believe that the VSX-6154 is a great addition to WDL Systems’ extensive line of PC/104 Single Board Computers,” remarks WDL Systems president, Mark Estill. “The small footprint, low-power and low-cost design of the VSX-6154 will provide many engineering benefits.”

On-board features of the VSX-6154 include soldered 128MB DDR2 system memory, TFT/LVDS LCD support (up to 1280x1024 resolution), Enhanced IDE (UltraDMA-100/66/33), 10/100Mbps Ethernet, 2 x USB 2.0, 4 serial ports, Parallel port, 16-bit GPIOs, 2MB SPI Flash, 2 watchdog timers and realtime clock with a lithium battery backup. The VSX-6154 offers extended operating temperature (-20°C to +70°C) and PC/104+ expansion capability.

For more information and to order the VSX-6154 from WDL Systems, please visit our Vortex86SX-6154 PC/104 SBC page.

VSX-6154 Features Include:
- 300MHz Vortex86SX System-On-Chip
- Low-power, fanless design
- 128MB DDR2 system memory
- 2 x USB 2.0, 4 x serial ports, Ethernet, IDE, Parallel, 16-bit GPIOs
- Extended operating temperature (-20°C to +70°C)
- Watch dog timers and Realtime Clock with Lithium Battery Backup
- Supports Linux and Windows CE
