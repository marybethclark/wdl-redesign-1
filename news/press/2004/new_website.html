EMJ America Inc Launches New “User Friendly” Website Design: Embedded Hardware shopping made easy

Pittsboro, NC - 29June 2004 – EMJ America Inc launches a new website design today. WDL Systems will be accessible from www.emjembedded.com and also through www.emj.com. The new site features improved navigation, with many helpful tools for researching EMJ’s extensive product line.

emjembedded.com offers a user friendly online shopping experience that guides the customer from Log In, all of the way to customer Check Out. Customers can edit their personal information, create a wishlist for future projects and buy products from a secure source.

With nearly 25 Vendors, EMJ strived to make this site informative and organized, so that users could easily find vendors or products. Mark Estill, Vice President of EMJ America Inc says, “This new site gives engineers one of the most comprehensive sites in the embedded business. Users will be able to find pricing, documentation and manuals in an instant."

The website was designed by EMJ's marketing department, with special programming provided by Blast Internet of Pittsboro, North Carolina. Blast is an experienced web development company that designs e-commerce, online marketing, and web applications using open source Platforms and tools. “Blast Internet Services was very pleased to be chosen to implement the new EMJ America website”, said Joe Canton, Blast Inc. Director of Development. “The new website effectively groups complex products, related products, and features together in a way that makes things easy to find.”


ABOUT WDL Systems
WDL Systems, a division of EMJ America Inc., distributes a full line of single board computers, Flash solutions, PC Card disk drives, PC Card readers, PC/104 add-on cards, PC/104 peripherals, displays, software and enclosures. EMJ is a publicly traded company with revenues of $150 million and can be found on the Toronto Stock Exchange under the symbol EMJ. (T.EMJ for Internet quote servers). Visit EMJ America online at www.emj.com.
