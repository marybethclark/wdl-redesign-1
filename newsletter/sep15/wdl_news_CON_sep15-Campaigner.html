Mini PCI Express
Gigabit Ethernet

MPG100 Series boards by Connect Tech are rugged, low cost Gigabit Ethernet Mini PCIe modules, ideal for adding extra ethernet capabilities to any system with a minimal increase in overall system size and power consumption. The wide operating temperature, latching Ethernet connector and cabling solution allows these modules to be used in harsh environments.

FEATURES :
• Add Ethernet to a system with minimal size increase
• Use in any Mini PCIe socket that supports PCIe
• Compatible with a variety of popular operating systems
• Easily integrated into most new or existing systems
• Extended Temperature Range -40°C to +85°C
