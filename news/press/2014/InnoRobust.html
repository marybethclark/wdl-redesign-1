WDL SYSTEMS PROVIDES INNOROBUST II 2.5" SSD by INNODISK

Pittsboro, NC - 11 September 2014 - WDL Systems proudly distributes InnoDisk’s InnoRobust II 2.5” SSD (Solid State Drive) Series high capacity flash memory from InnoDisk. InnoRobust II SSD provides extreme reliability and outstanding performance with data security features that are targeted in military, defense and aerospace applications. The InnoRobust feature set delivers all the operational robustness and ruggedness needed in defense industry environments.

InnoDisk’s InnoRobust II 2.5” SSD products are compliant with the Military Standard MIL-STD-810F/G, for operation in harsh environments. The InnoRobust II series is RoHS compliant with extended operating temperatures (40ºC to +85ºC) and built-in thermal sensors to ensure data reliability and to prevent failures due to overheating. For maximum protection, InnoDisk’s comprehensive data security suite for InnoRobust II SSDs includes: quick erase (QEraser), security erase (SEraser), write protect, and self-destruct functions. InnoRobust II SSD products feature high data transfer performance with sustained sequential read/write operations of up to 200/170 MB/s and IOPS 4KB read/write operations of up to 3200/1700 MB/s.

Features Include:
• Compliant with MIL-STD-810-F/G
• DataSecurity (QEraser/ SEraser/ Destroy/ Write Protect)
• Excellent data transfer rate
• Conformal coating (Optional)
• iCell technology for data protection
