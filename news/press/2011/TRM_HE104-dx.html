WDL SYSTEMS OFFERS HE104+DX POWER SUPPLIES FROM TRI-M TECHNOLOGIES

Pittsboro, NC – 11 May 2011 – WDL Systems proudly distributes High Efficiency PC/104 and PC/104-Plus Power Supplies and PC/104 Peripherals from Tri-M Technologies Inc.

The HE104+DX from Tri-M Technologies is a 108 Watt High Efficiency PC/104-Plus Power Supply designed for low noise embedded computer systems. The HE104+DX has a wide input range of 6-40V (>6:1) and is ideal for battery or unregulated input applications. The HE104+DX has heavy-duty transient suppressors (9000W on both main and secondary inputs) that clamp the input voltage to safe levels, while maintaining normal power supply operation.

The HE104+DX features a state-of-the-art Mosfet based design providing outstanding line and load regulation with efficiencies up to 90 percent. Organic Semiconductor Capacitors provide filtering that reduces ripple noises below 20mV. The low noise design makes the HE104+DX ideal for use aboard aircraft or military applications or wherever EMI or RFI must be minimized. The HE104+DX is now RoHS compliant.

HE104+DX Features Include:
 * 108 Watt Power Supply
* 6V to 40V DC input range
* +3.3V, +5V, +12V, -12C DC output
* High efficiency up to 90%
* PC/104+ (PC/104 & PCI-104)
* Extended temperature: -40°C to +85°C
