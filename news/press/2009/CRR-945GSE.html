WDL SYSTEMS NOW SHIPPING Cool RoadRunner-945GSE from LiPPERT

Pittsboro, NC – 9 November 2009 – WDL Systems is now shipping the Cool RoadRunner-945GSE (formerly named "Cool RoadRunner-Atom") from LiPPERT. This PC/104- Plus CPU Board delivers high computing performance while consuming only little power. The Cool RoadRunner-945GSE’s Atom processor was created with the most advanced semi-conductor technology and is especially suitable for low-power embedded devices.

The Cool RoadRunner-945GSE operates from a single 5V supply. To conserve even more power, ACPI, advanced power management, and PCIe power management are supported. The Cool RoadRunner-945GSE is available in the standard temperature range (-20°C ~ +60°C), or extended temperature (-40°C ~ +85°C). Cooling solutions are available for both temperature ranges. Supported operating systems are Windows, Windows Embedded, Linux and QNX.

Key features of the the Cool RoadRunner-945GSE include:

• Intel® Atom™ N270 Processor
• Up to 2 Gbyte soldered DDR2-RAM
• SXGA: 2048 x 1536 pixel
• CRT and LVDS
• Gigabit Ethernet
• 8 x USB 2.0
• 2 x RS232/RS422/RS485
• 2 GB Solid State Disk (SSD)
• HD audio
• 2 x SATA
• Low Power consumption (12.5 watts)
• Passive cooling possible
• Optionally extended temperature range (-40°C ~ +85°C)
• Lithium Battery for RTC backup
• Flash BIOS
• Watchdog
