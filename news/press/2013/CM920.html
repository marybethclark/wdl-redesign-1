WDL SYSTEMS NOW SHIPPING COREMODULE 920 by ADLINK

Pittsboro, NC - 24 January 2013 - WDL Systems is now shipping the CoreModule® 920 Extreme Rugged™ PCI/104-Express 3rd Generation Intel® Core™ Processor Single Board Computer from Ampro by ADLINK™.

The CoreModule 920 is a PCI/104-Express SBC (Type 1) based on the 3rd generation Intel® Core™ i7-3517UE processor and provides the highest level of performance for Ampro by ADLINK’s PC/104 product family. The PCI/104-Express stackable form factor supports both PCIe and PCI bus connectivity and allows customers to build low power solutions for space constrained, extreme rugged environments.

The CoreModule 920 is equipped with the Intel® Core™ i7-3517UE processor and Mobile Intel® QM67 Express Chipset to provide support for up to 4 GB DDR3 industrial grade soldered SDRAM at 1066/1333 MHz, 18/24-bit LVDS, HDMI and VGA display output, 8GB of industrial grade SSD, 2x SATA 6 Gb/s, 4x USB and 2x GbE. Power consumption can be reduced to 10 watts by reducing the CPU speed or running only a single core for low power applications. The CoreModule 920 is particularly well suited to meet the size, performance, temperature range, quality, and reliability demands of mission critical land and airborne transport applications.

CoreModule 920 Features:
• Rugged by Design Dual-core 3rd generation Intel® Core™ processor
• Up to 4GB industrial grade solder down ECC DDR3 memory
• Up to 8GB industrial grade Solid State Disk
• Up to 3 simultaneous digital displays
• Extended Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
• 50% thicker PCB for high vibration environments
• One PCIe x16 (Gen 3) and 1 x4 or 4 x1 (Gen 2)
• SATA 6 Gb/s x2, GbE x2, USB 2.0 x2, and Serial ports x2
• PCI/104-Express Type 1 and PCI/104 expansion interfaces
