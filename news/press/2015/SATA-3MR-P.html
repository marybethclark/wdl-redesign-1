WDL Systems Proudly Distributes InnoDisk’s 2.5” SATA SSD 3MR-P

Pittsboro, NC - 22 April 2015 - WDL Systems proudly distributes InnoDisk’s 2.5” SATA SSD 3MR-P for excellent SSD performance and reliability in extreme conditions. 2.5” SATA SSD 3MR-P is compliant with MIL-STD-810F/G standards and E-Mark/SAE J113/ISO 7637-2 for EMI resistance, and is equipped with a ruggedized hardware design for withstanding harsh environments.  2.5” SATA SSD 3MR-P is developed with Innodisk owned technical know-how to ensure data integrity and highest levels of reliability.

2.5” SATA SSD 3MR-P Key Features and Highlights:
• Capacities up to 256GB in SLC and up to 1TB in MLC
• Compliant with MIL-STD-810-F/G
• Thermal sensor for temperature control
• Quick Eraser, Security Eraser, Write Protect & Destroy functionality
• iSMART Utility provides real time disk health and product lifespan
• iCell Technology prevents data loss due to abnormal power failure
• Operating Temperatures from -40°C to +85°C
