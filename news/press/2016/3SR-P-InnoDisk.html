WDL Systems Proudly Distributes Innodisk's 2.5” SATA SSD 3SR-P with InnoRobust Features

Pittsboro, NC - 9 September 2016 - WDL Systems proudly distributes Innodisk's rugged 2.5” SATA SSD 3SR-P Series, a SATA III 6.0Gb/s Flash based disk delivering excellent performance and reliability. The 2.5” SATA SSD 3SR-P features the InnoRobust SSD Series Data Security Functions of QEraser/ SEraser/ Destroy and Write Protect which can be triggered by both hardware and software approaches. Its ruggedized hardware design makes 2.5” SATA SSD 3SR-P ideal for applications in harsh environments, while Innodisk owned technology ensures it boasts the highest levels of data integrity and reliability.

Innodisk 2.5” SATA SSD 3SR-P comes in capacities up to 256GB and is available with an Industrial Grade Operating Temperature of -40°C ~ +85°C. It is shock resistant up to 1500g/0.5ms and vibration resistant 20G @7~2000Hz with a MTBF of 3 million hours.

3SR-P Series Features Include:

  • Compliant with MIL-STD-810-F/G
  • Data Security (QEraser/ SEraser/ Destroy/ Write Protect)
  • High random R/W performance
  • iSMART disk health monitoring
  • Intelligent error recovery system
  • iData Guard/iCell technology for data protection
  • Conformal coating (optional)
