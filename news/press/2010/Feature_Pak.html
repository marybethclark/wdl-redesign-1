From WDL eNewsletter on Connectivity

 Sent May 18, 2010

FeaturePak is a mezzanine-style embedded system expansion standard from Connect Tech Inc. designed to work with existing standards such as PC/104, SUMIT, Qseven, ETX, XTX, COM Express, etc. FeaturePak allows system design reconfiguration using off the shelf modules based on an open standard.

Xtreme/FP
4-Port Multiport Serial and Digital IO FeaturePak Module • Smaller than a credit card at 42mm x 65mm in size
• Low-cost connector for I/O interfaces
• Adds four high speed serial ports to your FeaturePak compatible baseboard or single board computer.


PCI Express to FeaturePak Adapter
Converts any FeaturePak Module to PCI Express
