TWELVE REASONS TO VISIT WDL SYSTEMS AT AUVSI 2012

Pittsboro, NC – 6 July 2012 – For four exciting days, the Mandalay Bay Convention Center will become the world’s largest robotics playground! Unmanned systems and their supporters will be invading Las Vegas for AUVSI’s Unmanned Systems North America 2012.

WDL Systems will be attending AUVSI (Association for Unmanned Vehicle Systems International) for the first time in August 2012. AUVSI’s Unmanned Systems North America 2012 is the premier forum for reviewing, assessing and discussing the current state of the unmanned systems market. The event brings together the key leaders and decision-makers in the industry. Officials from government and user organizations around the world attend and participate in the symposium program.
