WDL SYSTEMS OFFERS COM EXPRESS® TYPE 10 MINI CARRIER BOARD BY CTI

Pittsboro, NC - 30 August 2013 - WDL Systems offers Connect Tech's COM Express® Type 10 Mini Carrier Board. Extremely compact in size, the COM Express Type 10 Mini Carrier Board from Connect Tech features rugged, locking connectors for ultimate durability. Ideal for space constrained applications, harsh environments and demanding conditions, the Mini Carrier Board also supports extended temperature ranges of -40°C to +85°C.

“Aside from being extremely compact we're excited about the new processor options that will soon be available such as Intel Bay Trail and AMD Kabini solutions that will provide significant horse power while maintaining really low power solutions,” states Michele Kasza, Vice President Sales of Connect Tech.

Matching the dimensions of the COM Express Type 10 specification, Connect Tech's COM Express® Type 10 Mini Carrier Board is ideal for applications where size, weight, and power (SWaP) are a concern. Connect Tech's COM Express Type 10 Carrier Boards are available off the shelf for proof of concept or production purposes. For specialized requirements, Connect Tech offers modified COTS solutions to create an exact technology fit, shorten the design cycle, and reduce development costs.

COM Express® Type 10 Mini Carrier Board Features:
o Extremely small size, 84mm x 55mm
o Rugged, locking pin header connectors
o 2 x mini PCIe, mSATA, SATA, 2 x GBE, 6 x USB, LVDS, DisplayPort/HDMI/DVI/VGA, HD Audio, 2 x RS-232/422/485
o Extended Temperature Range
o Multi-vendor, multi-CPU support
