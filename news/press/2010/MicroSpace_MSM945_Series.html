WDL SYSTEMS PROUDLY DISTRIBUTES MICROSPACE® MSM945 BOARDS FROM KONTRON

Pittsboro, NC – 25 May 2010 – WDL Systems proudly distributes MICROSPACE® MSM945 Boards from Kontron for building high performance embedded PC applications. MICROSPACE® MSM945 features standard PC interfaces plus Ethernet LAN, optional DVI and LVDS interfaces (dual screen) and an audio controller. Equipped with the CPU Intel® CoreTM2 Duo L7400 in the DIGITAL-LOGIC smartModule SMX945 with COMexpress offers best performance in the PC/104 form factor. The optional PCI/104-Express bus (PCI & PCI Express) and 6 USB interfaces are available as functional extensions. By means of a PC/104-Plus ISA bridge card, an 8 bit ISA bus can be emulated from the 32 bit PCI bus.

Applications for the MSM945 include:
• HD Video streaming
• Dual screen applications
• Video monitoring with Image processing
• Calculation-intensive data processing

The MICROSPACE PCI/104-Express MSM945 MSM945/L/P is a miniaturized modular device incorporating the major elements of a PC/AT compatible computer including standard PC/AT compatible elements, such as: Powerful Core Duo, Core Solo or Core 2 Duo processors; BIOS ROM; SODIMM 200pin; DMA, LPT1 parallel port; COM1-, COM2- RS232 serial port 16C550 comp; AT-IDE hard disk interface; 2x S-ATA 1.5GB/s HDD; DVI, VGA, LVDS Video; AC97-V2.3 Sound and 100/10-Base T-LAN.

SMX945 smartModuleExpress945 is a miniaturized PC system-on-chip unit incorporating the major elements of a PC/AT compatible computer, such as: Powerful Core Duo, Core 2 Duo or Core Solo CPU; AMI BIOS; DDR2 SODIMM; Dual 220pin connectors; COMexpress Bus Type 2; 32bit PCI interface; IDE port; Up to 6 PCI Express general purpose lanes; PCI Express Graphics (PEG) slot; Up to 8 USB 2.0 ports; Powerful internal Graphic controller GMA950; HD Audio; Single Ethernet interface.

For more information and to order MICROSPACE® MSM945 Boards from Kontron, please visit PCI/104-Express Boards on the WDL Systems website: Visit our Vendor Page for all Kontron products. Email: sales@wdlsystems.com.

WDL Systems is a privately owned company that distributes a full line of single board computers, Flash solutions, PC Card disk drives, PC Card readers, PC/104 add-on cards, PC/104 peripherals, alphanumeric displays, embedded software, and PC/104 enclosures. Other product specialties include multi-platform internetworking software and Internet security software. WDL Systems distributes the latest technology to provide customers with Commercial-Off-The-Shelf (COTS) products for all embedded requirements.
