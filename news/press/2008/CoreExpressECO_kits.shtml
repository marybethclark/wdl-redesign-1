WDL SYSTEMS DISTRIBUTES CoreExpress™-ECO EVALUATION KIT from LiPPERT

Pittsboro, NC – 13 November 2008 – WDL Systems is now shipping the CoreExpress™-ECO Evaluation Kit from LiPPERT. The CoreExpress-ECO Evaluation Kit contains all hardware and software necessary to test and evaluate the CoreExpress-ECO Computer-On-Module. The Evaluation Kit comes in a sturdy carrying case and contains the CoreExpress-ECO module mounted on a carrier board, a 13.3" TFT display panel, a complete documentation set, all required cables and a power supply. Windows® XP Embedded or Linux™ is pre-installed on the included CompactFlash disk. Each kit is covered by three free hours of direct manufacturer support.

"System integrators will profit from the ready for use CoreExpress™-ECO Evaluation Kit," says WDL President, Mark Estill. " Within its sturdy carrying case, there is everything needed to get a prototype up and running in no time."

The CoreExpress™-ECO works with Intel's Atom™ processor, featuring an extremely low power requirement of typically 5 watts at 1.6 GHz. The 58 mm x 65 mm size makes the CoreExpress-ECO the smallest embedded PC module available.

CoreExpress™-ECO Features:

- Intel® Atom™ Z530 (1.6 GHz) or Z510 (1.1 GHz)
- up to 2 GB soldered DDR2 RAM
- built-in graphics processor
- LVDS (1376 x 768 px)
- SDVO (1280 x 1024 px)
- H.264- und MPEG2/4-decoding
- HV-1 and WMV9 codecs
- HD audio
- 2 PCI Express-x1-lanes
- 8 USB 2.0 ports
- parallel ATA
- SDIO/MMC, SMBus, GMBus/DDC, and LPC-Bus
- LiPPERT Enhanced Management Technology. (LEMT)
