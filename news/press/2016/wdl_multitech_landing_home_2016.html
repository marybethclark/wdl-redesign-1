MultiConnect® mDOT™ Long Range RF Modules


Buy Now

MultiConnect® mDot™ are LoRaWAN™ Ready, Low-Power Wide Area Network (LPWAN) RF modules, capable of 2-way communication over distances in excess of 10 miles / 16 km with excellent noise immunity and the ability to penetrate deep into buildings and through structures.

  • FCC/CE end-certified with ARM® mbed™ libraries for developers
  • 2-way duplex communication, ideal for emergency or mission-critical applications
  • Multiple I/O interfaces for most any "Thing"
  • LoRaWAN data rates 293 bps - 20 Kbps + FSK up to 300 Kbps 
MultiConnect® mCard™ Gateway Accessory Cards


Buy Now

MultiConnect® mCard™ accessory cards allow the addition of application specific interfaces to gateway devices.

  • Multi-Function Serial (both DTE and DCE variants)
  • General Purpose Input Output (GPIO)
  • LoRaWAN Long Range, Low Power Wireless Access Network
