WDL NOW SHIPPING SP-860 ARM-BASED SMART PANEL FROM ADLINK

Pittsboro, NC – 15 May 2012 – WDL Systems is now shipping the SP-860 ARM-based Smart Panel from ADLINK Technology, Inc. The SP-860 Smart Panel is a powerful and richly featured system-on-display, combining an LCD panel, CPU, and touch screen in a single compact unit.

The SP-860 Smart Panel features a 4:3 TFT-LCD Display and TI® - Sitara AM3517 Cortex™ A8 Processor. The SP-860 includes built-in Wi-Fi and Blue Tooth SIP module, stackable expansion capability, and supports two LAN ports. Optional items include four wire resistive touch sensor, and a high brightness, sunlight readable display version. The SP-860 Smart Panel supports Linux 2.6.32, Windows CE 6.0, and Android 2.3.4 operating systems.

ADLINK Smart Panel products comprise highly integrated, ultrathin, and flexible designs that are ready for development. ADLINK Smart Panels are ideal for panel computing applications such as factory automation equipment, medical care systems, transportation systems, multimedia navigation devices, advertising systems, and interactive kiosks.

SP-860 Smart Panel Features Include:
• All-in-one design with TI - Sitara AM3517 Cortex A8 Processor
• Built in Wi-Fi+ BT SIP module
• Stackable expansion capability
• OS: Linux 2.6.32; WinCE 6.0; Android 2.3.4
• Supports 2 LAN ports
• High brightness, sunlight readable LCD display options
• Optional four-wire resistive touch sensor
