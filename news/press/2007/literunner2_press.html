WDL SYSTEMS IS NOW SHIPPING THE COOL LITERUNNER 2 FROM LIPPERT

Pittsboro, NC – 29 January 2007 – WDL Systems is now shipping the Cool LiteRunner 2 from LiPPERT. The Cool LiteRunner 2 is an affordable PC/104 CPU Board, powered by an AMD Geode™ GX 466@0.9W Pentium-compatible processor running at 333 MHz. The Cool LiteRunner 2 features 256 MB RAM, full AC97 sound, 16 bit ISA bus capability and four USB 2.0 host ports.

“We’re pleased to offer the Cool LiteRunner 2 as the enhanced successor of the Cool LiteRunner from LiPPERT,” says WDL Systems president, Mark Estill. “Developers are sure to appreciate the features of this highly versatile and affordable PC/104 CPU Board.”

For more information and to order the Cool LiteRunner 2 from WDL Systems, please visit our website's Cool LiteRunner 2 page.

Cool LiteRunner 2 Features Include:
- AMD Geode™ GX 466@0.9W Processor
- 256 MB soldered RAM
- miniPCI slot
- CompactFlash socket
- 2 x 10/100 BaseT Ethernet
- 4 x USB 2.0
- 3 x serial RS232/422/485
- General purpose I/O
- Power consumption: ~6.5 watts
- No heatsink needed
- Extended temperature range -40 to +85 °C
- GoldCap for backup
- Flash BIOS
- RoHS compliant
