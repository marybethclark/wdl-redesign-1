WDL SYSTEMS NOW SHIPPING COMe-bSC6 FROM KONTRON

Pittsboro, NC - 3 December 2013 - WDL Systems is now shipping Kontron's COMe-bSC6 family of COM Express® basic (formerly known as ETXexpress®) Pin-out Type 6 modules. Measuring just 125 mm x 95 mm, COMe-bSC6 modules are equipped with USB 3.0 for increased display output for interfaces requiring multiple display solutions and cost-efficient high-data transmission. COMe-bSC6 modules are ideal for applications such as industrial vision applications utilizing high-definition and high-speed video technology.

Kontron's COMe-bSC6 family is available with various CPUs: from Celeron®, to dual-core CPUs Core™ i3/i5/i7, to quad-core CPU Core™ i7-2715QE; allowing the COMe-bS6 series to meet many individual performance needs with revolutionary performance values, both for CPU and GPU rankings.

COMe-bSC6 Features:
  • Quad Core Performance and USB 3.0 for COM Express®
  • COM Express® basic Pin-out Type 6
  • Intel® Core™ i7, i5 and i3 with QM67
  • Up to 16 GB DDR3 with ECC support
  • New DDI graphic interfaces and optional USB 3.0
  • SPI, LPC, SMB, Fast I²C, Staged Watchdog, RTC, MARS
  • Dimensions: 125 x 95 mm
  • Windows® 7, Windows® Vista, Windows® XP, WES 7, Linux, VxWorks
  • Operating Temperature: 0°C to 60°C
