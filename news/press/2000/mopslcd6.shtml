WDL Systems Shipping the World's Most Integrated PC/104 Board-- the MOPSlcd6

Apex, NC- 5 January 2000 -- WDL Systems is shipping the world's most highly integrated PC/104 board-- the MOPSlcd6 from JUMPtec (now known as Kontron).

The MOPSlcd6 is low in power consumption but packs Pentium processing speeds, USB, fast Ethernet, LCD/CRT support, and an optional PC/104 Plus Bus, all on a PC/104 industry standard board.

The MOPSlcd6 is perfect for kiosks, gaming applications, and frame grabbers. It is also ideal for applications requiring powerful processing speeds, such as test and measurement systems; ideal for applications requiring fast Ethernet; and, because of its low power draw, it is also ideal for use in battery powered systems.

EMJ Vice President Mark Estill says of MOPSlcd6, "This is a real break-through product from JUMPtec, and we are thrilled to be offering it. For the first time, developers can get one PC/104 board that offers Pentium performance, Ethernet, video, USB, and even the PC/104 Plus Bus."

FEATURES
The JUMPtec MOPSlcd6 offers Pentium 166MHz MMX performance, LCD/VGA support, USB and 10/100 BaseT Ethernet in the PC/104 form factor. Its power consumption is less than 8 Watts, and no heat sink or fan is necessary. The MOPSlcd6 has an onboard PCI C&T Graphic Controller that allows support for mono-LCD, TFT and STN displays with resolutions up to SXGA (1280 x 1024). It provides a harddrive interface, floppy interface, parallel port, two serial ports, a keyboard controller and a real-time clock. It ships with no DRAM or Flash onboard. A SODIMM socket on board allows for up to 64MB SDRAM. Flash can only be added with add-on cards. For more information visit: http://www.emjembedded.com/products/single/pc104.html
