WDL Systems and Mindready Solutions Inc. Offer In-Depth Training On Powerful Interface Standard

Pittsboro, NC – 15 January 2002 – WDL Systems and Mindready Solutions announce a series of jointly-sponsored North Carolina training classes on the technology and applications of IEEE-1394/Firewire, a powerful new interface standard.

IIEEE-1394/FireWire enables extremely fast data transfer, up to 400-Mbps, between a computer system and a connected device. It is used in a variety of applications - process control applications for factories, automobile and aerospace communication links, telecommunication switch backplane, digital camera applications, and more.

Mindready is renowned as a world leader in communication solutions for embedded systems. The company has pioneered the development of 1394-based discoveries, including a comprehensive range of software, hardware and professional services for the telecommunications, aerospace, and automotive industries.

Each two-day training class, which will take place at Mindready’s RTP headquarters, will provide all the necessary tools to understand the IEEE-1394 Standard. "This technology is revolutionary. It advances the design and integration of heterogeneous real-time computer systems while reducing overall project costs." says EMJ Embedded System's Director of Marketing.

For current information on Mindready's training sessions, visit the training section of Mindready's website.
