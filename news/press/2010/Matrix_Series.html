From WDL eNewsletter on Compact PCs

Sent March 4, 2010

The Matrix Series (MXC-2000 & MXE-1000) are ADLINK's revolutionary rugged I/O and fanless Platforms-Matrix featuring the Intel® Atom™ N270 1.6 GHz processor + 945GSE chipset. These platforms combine ADLINK's expertise in x86 platform design, versatile I/O function development, and thermal design to push fanless systems to a higher standard, including a temperature range of - 20 degrees to + 70 degrees, 5 G vibration, and cable-free durable structure.

MXC-2000 Series
Configurable Controller with PCI/PCIe Slots
• Two PCI/PCIe expansion slots
• Built-in 9 VDC to 32 VDC wide-range DC power input
• Optional on-board 16-CH isolated DI and DO

MXE-1000 Series
Embedded Controller with Integrated I/O
• Dedicated GBE and 1394b ports for camera connectivity
• Built-in 6 VDC to 36 VDC wide-range DC power input
• One internal PCIe Mini Card socket with an USIM socket
