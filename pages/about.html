ABOUT WDL SYSTEMS



Since 1990, WDL Systems distributes a full line of solutions for Industrial IoT and Edge Computing applications. Products include industrial PC systems, computer on modules, single board computers, industrial flash storage, 3D Printers, wireless data infrastructure, as well as measurement and automation.


WDL Systems distributes the latest technology to provide customers with Commercial-Off-The-Shelf (COTS) products for all embedded and industrial requirements and markets including Military, Aerospace, Transportation, Medical, Energy, Oil & Gas, Infotainment and Smart Factory.


WDL Systems' website provides online ordering for standard products in addition to product information and technical support. WDL Systems also offers integration services.


WDL Systems is the Embedded Products Source.


PROFILE



In business since 1990, WDL Systems distributes a full line of Single Board Computers, Flash solutions, PC Card Disk Drives, PC Card Readers, PC/104 Add-on Cards, PC/104 Peripherals, Alphanumeric Displays, Embedded Modem solutions, Embedded Software, and PC/104 Enclosures. Other product specialties include Multi-Platform Internetworking Software and Internet Security Software. WDL Systems distributes the latest technology to provide customers with Commercial-Off-The-Shelf (COTS) products for all embedded requirements.


Through our headquarters in Pittsboro, North Carolina, we support our national and international sales with toll-free phone lines, a large inventory, and same-day shipping. Developers calling WDL Systems have access to a comprehensive selection of embedded products, a knowledgeable sales staff, technical support, and assistance with configuration and integration issues.

ENVIRONMENTAL



In addition to recycling waste products such as paper, cardboard, glass, newspaper, and plastic on a daily basis, WDL Systems' building in Pittsboro, North Carolina was specifically designed to be environmentally sound; while meeting the needs of the company. We chose to install a geothermal heating and air conditioning system, named by the Environmental Protection Agency as the most energy-efficient, environmentally clean, and cost-efficient space conditioning system available. It is anticipated that this system will reduce WDL Systems' energy consumption by 40% as compared to air source heat pumps. Other energy-saving features include argon gas filled windows to increase efficiency, large windows to bring in more natural light and a planned wastewater treatment system using a constructed wetland.


WDL Systems, has previous knowledge of innovative wastewater plans. In 1995, we were involved (as EMJ) in the restoration of an abandoned building on the shores of Jordan Lake. A traditional septic system could not be used, so reclamation of the building could only occur when a researcher from NC State University designed an unique water recycling system that used constructed wetlands to cleanse the water. See http://waterrecycling.com for more information on this water recycling system.
