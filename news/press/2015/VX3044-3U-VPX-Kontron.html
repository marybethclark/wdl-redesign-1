WDL Systems Features Kontron’s VX3044 3U VPX SBC

Pittsboro, NC - 1 October 2015 - WDL Systems proudly offers Kontron’s VX3044 3U VPX 3rd Gen Core™ i7 Quad-Core™ Single Board Computer - the perfect blade for High Performance Embedded Computing (HPEC).

VX3044 is a rugged SBC with multiple 10G Ethernet and Intel® HD Graphics 4000 providing ultimate performance in the Military, Aerospace and Transportation markets. Based on Intel®’s 3rd Generation Core™ i7 high performance computing architecture and supported by Kontron Long Term Supply (LTS) services, VX3044 offers the best feature set.

Used in clusters, VX3044 provides the highest computing density in 3U VPX form factor. Backed by a powerful I/O backbone (gen3 PCIe and Ethernet 10G), switch boards for PCIe (VX3905) and Ethernet (VX3910, VX3920) plus turnkey pre-installed OS,  VX3044 solutions complete the quickest route from the lab to the field for HPEC applications. VX3044 is equipped with Kontron VXFabric™ technology for implementing the TCP/IP protocol over the PCI Express infrastructure.

VX3044’s SWaP-C design is tailored to support systems with high size, weight and power and cooling demands. VX3044 is compatible with all Kontron VPX building blocks (payload boards, carriers, switches, backplanes, OS and drivers) and offers backward compatibility with previous product generations (VX303x SBCs), allowing customers to write the code once, with the ability to run on all the product lines. Designed for PCIe Gen3 8GB/s connectivity with I/O extensions at peak bandwidth, VX3044 features 4x SATA ports for secure RAID storage applications.

Kontron Extended Life Cycle offers a 15-year life cycle organization on top of the Intel® embedded product line silicon life cycle. With Long Term Supply (LTS), VX3044 is the ideal candidate for long term programs in compact high performance parallel computing solutions.

VX3044 FEATURES:

  • Intel® Core™ i7 - 3612QE - Quad-Core™ Rugged SBC
  • Dual Ethernet up to 10G
  • Intel® HD Graphics 4000
  • PCI Express Gen 3.0, with Kontron VXFabric™
  • Up to 16 GB SDRAM with ECC and 32 GB Soldered SATA NAND Flash
  • Turnkey Evaluation/Development Platforms
  • XMC with front and rear I/O Support
  • Extended Life Cycle, Air-Cooled and Conduction-Cooled Versions
