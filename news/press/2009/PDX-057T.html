WDL SYSTEMS ANNOUNCES AVAILABILITY OF INDUSTRIAL PANEL PC from ICOP

Pittsboro, NC – 2 May 2009 – WDL Systems announces availability of the new PDX-057T Panel PC from ICOP Technology. Built around DMP’s Vortex86DX 32-bit, x86-compatible SoC (system-on-chip), the PDX-057T has a 5.7-inch resistive touchscreen LCD TFT display, up to 512MB of RAM, CompactFlash Type I/II and microSD storage, and a Mini PCI slot. Weighing less than a pound, the PDX-057T features 3 USB ports, optional audio output, RS232 port, 10/100 Ethernet port, and a PS/2 port for keyboard/mouse connection.

The PDX-057T panel PC also sports a connector for an optional antenna, relevant when a wireless LAN card is installed into the device's Mini-PCI slot. The standard version of the PDX-057T accepts 5VDC input power from an external 15W adapter. Each display is built into a sturdy, VESA-compatible enclosure. The PDX-057T runs Linux, Windows CE, Windows XP, and DOS.

“The PDX-057T from ICOP is a game-changer in the industrial computing world,” comments WDL Systems technologist, Donnie Springfield. “This revolutionary Panel PC is low power, lightweight and comes in under the critical $400 threshold.”

The low-power Vortex86DX is a 90nm process SoC in a 27 x 27mm package and consumes under 2W while running at up to 1GHz. The Vortex86DX has 256KB of embedded L2 cache, and supports widely available DDR2 memory.

Features for the PDX-057T include:

• 1GHz Vortex86DX SoC
• 5.7" TFT LCD with resistive touchscreen and VESA-compatible chassis
• 256MB or 512MB of DDR2 RAM
• CompactFlash Type I/II and microSD slots
• 10/100 Ethernet
• Other I/O: 3x USB 2.0, 1x RS232, 1 x PS/2, Audio Out (optional)
• Mini PCI expansion slot
• -5 to 60 deg. C (23 to 140 deg. F) operating temperature
• Power requirements: 5VDC (PDX-057T-5) or 8VDC to 35VDC (PDX-057T-8)
• Dimensions: 5.98 x 4.41 x 1.3 inches (152 x 112 x 33mm)
• Weight: 0.82 pounds (374g)
