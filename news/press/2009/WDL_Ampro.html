WDL SYSTEMS NOW DISTRIBUTING PRODUCTS from AMPRO by ADLINK

Pittsboro, NC – 5 January 2009 – WDL Systems is now distributing products from Ampro by ADLINK, an industry leader in embedded systems and the PC/104 form factor. Ampro by ADLINK PC/104 and PC/104-Plus CPU Boards now shipping from WDL Systems include the CoreModule 800 PCI-104, CoreModule 620, and CoreModule 420. Ampro's ETX, XTX and COM Express Computer-on-Modules and LittleBoard EBX, ReadyBoard EPIC and MightyBoard Mini-ITX Single Board Computers have also been added to WDL Systems' extensive embedded systems linecard.

"The diverse SBC and COM offerings from Ampro by ADLINK provide a wealth of new high quality options for WDL Systems customers," says WDL Systems Technologist, Donnie Springfield. "Ampro products can be easily optimized for a wide range of embedded system applications requiring ruggedness, reliability and modularity."

The CoreModule PC/104 Single Board Computers from Ampro by ADLINK are complete embedded single board computers in PC/104 and PC/104-Plus form-factors that feature: long-term-availability of 486, Celeron and Celeron M CPUs; a full complement of PC core logic functions, including Ethernet, video, IDE, USB, and serial and parallel ports; PC/104 (ISA) or PCI-104 (PCI) expandability with optional MiniModule PC/104-Plus (ISA & PCI) bridge support; smallest size, thick, rugged and extended temperature options.

The CoreModule 620 Geode PC/104 Single Board Computer is a highly integrated device for rugged embedded applications in a compact size and RoHS compliance. The CoreModule 620 boasts AMD Geode performance with advanced networking, low power and all of the PC-compatible component subsystems.

CoreModule 620 Features :

• 500MHz AMD Geode LX800
• Up to 1GB DDR333 SODIMM
• PC/104 (ISA) Interface
• CompactFlash Socket on primary IDE
• (2) Serial Ports, (1) is RS232/422/485
• Floppy - Supports (1) or (2) drives, shared with parallel
• (2) USB 2.0 ports, PS/2 Keyboard/Mouse ports
• 10/100BaseT Ethernet Interface
• Supports resolutions up to 1600x1200, up to 4MB UMA frame buffer, supports TTL LCD panels
• -40° to +85°C extended temperature option
