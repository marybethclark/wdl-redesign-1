Swissbit’s new durabit™ X-60m Series SATA III (6.0Gb/s) Industrial mSATA and M.2 SSD modules deliver impressive IOPS rate and highest endurance by combining MLC Flash technology with an high end controller architecture, firmware and an optimized configuration.

durabit™ SSDs are designed for all industrial, Netcom and automotive applications requiring high data transfer rates up to 525 MB/s in sequential access and 79,000 IOPS in 4KB random access. In addition, they offer a wide range of features such as Swissbit’s proven Power Fail Safety, ATA security feature set, Data Care Management tools for sustained retention, advanced Wear Leveling & Bad Block management and in-field firmware update functionality.
