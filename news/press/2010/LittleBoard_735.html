WDL SYSTEMS NOW SHIPPING LITTLEBOARD 735 from AMPRO by ADLINK

Pittsboro, NC – 15 January 2010 – WDL Systems proudly distributes the Ampro by ADLINK™ LittleBoard™ 735 EBX Single Board Computer featuring the ultra low power Intel® Atom™ N270 processor at 1.6 GHz and 945GSE chipset (based on the Navy Pier platform). The Ampro by ADLINK™ Extreme Rugged™ LittleBoard 735 SBC is designed to tolerate shock, vibration, humidity, and temperature extremes.

The LittleBoard™ 735 is a full-featured EBX form factor single-board solution measuring 5.75 x 8.0 inches (146 x 203 mm) that provides a feature-rich mid-performance SBC for a wide range of legacy-friendly applications. Featuring the PC/104-Plus expansion interface for legacy PC/104 and PC/104-Plus modules, this SBC is well-rounded with dual Ethernet, CRT and Flat Panel Video, 4 serial ports, 4 USB ports, SATA and IDE interfaces, Compact Flash socket, Mini-PCI Express socket, high definition audio and GPIO support as an easy upgrade from older EBX SBCs.

LittleBoard 735 EBX Single Board Computer Features:

• DDR2 533 SODIMM socket for up to 2 GB of RAM
• Independent 10/100BaseT and 10/100/1000BaseT Ethernet ports
• Supports Intel’s integrated chipset Graphics Media Accelerator (GMA)
• Two SATA ports
• Six USB 2.0 ports
• PCIe Mini Card socket for 802.11 wireless or other plug-in cards
• Four serial ports
• EPP/ECP parallel port
• PS/2 keyboard and mouse interfaces
• One Ultra/DMA 33/66/100 IDE controller
* Compact Flash socket
• ITE IT8888 PCI-to-ISA Bridge

For more information and to order the Ampro by ADLINK™ LittleBoard™ 735, please visit the LittleBoard 735 section inside Single Board Computers on the WDL Systems website. Visit our Vendor Page for more Ampro by ADLINK products. Email: sales@wdlsystems.com.
