WDL Systems Is On Fire With Mindready’s IEEE –1394 Technology

 FireWire™ Interface Technology Allows High-Speed, Real-Time Communications in Embedded Systems Applications

Apex, NC – 28 August, 2001 – WDL Systems expands its roster of world-class embedded products with the addition of IEEE-1394 technology from Mindready Solutions Inc. (TSE:MNY). Officially today, EMJ distributes Mindready’s comprehensive portfolio of hardware and software throughout North America.

IEEE-1394/FireWire® is a powerful standard for a new interface technology that enables extremely fast data transfer, up to 400-Mbps, between a computer system and a connected device. It is used in a variety of applications - process control applications for factories, automobile and aerospace communication links, telecommunication switch backplane, digital camera applications, and more. Mindready will soon release products based on the new powerful communication bus, 1394b, a new version of the IEEE-1394 standard, that will support greater bandwidth (first up to 800 Mbps), greater distance (up to 100 meters node-to-node distance) and greater price savings.

Mindready is a world pioneer in developing 1394-based advanced solutions, including a comprehensive range of software, hardware and professional services for the telecommunications, aerospace, and automotive industries. The company’s IEEE-1394 products offer easy-to-use interface connections, high-speed transfer rates, scalability, and real-time data processing. Mindready's hardware communication adapters facilitate the design and integration of heterogeneous real-time computer systems while reducing overall project costs.

The Mindready hardware product line includes IEEE-1394 boards for PC/104+, PCI, CompactPCI and VME systems. Mindready’s software includes SedNet Pro Stack and SedNet’s API for Windows 2000, 95, NT, VxWorks, and LynxOS operating systems.

EMJ Vice President Mark Estill says, "IEEE-1394 is cutting edge technology. It allows data transfer rates that we couldn't imagine for embedded systems only a few years ago. I am thrilled to work with such an industry leader as Mindready in bringing FireWire technology to the embedded market."


ABOUT MINDREADY SOLUTIONS INC.
Mindready is a leading supplier of real-time communications, automation and test engineering solutions for embedded systems for the telecommunications, automotive and aerospace industries. Recognized as a leader in IEEE-1394-based products and professional services, Mindready offers complete innovative solutions based on this technology. Mindready’s expertise covers design and development of real-time applications, as well as complete test engineering solutions for complex embedded systems. Mindready’s clients include Nortel Networks, Hewlett-Packard, Wind River, Boeing, Fujitsu, Maxtor, Xerox and Texas Instruments.

More information about Mindready and its products and services can be found at www.mindready.com.


ABOUT WDL Systems
WDL Systems, a division of EMJ America Inc., distributes a full line of single board computers, CompactPCI solutions, Flash solutions, PC Card disk drives, PC Card readers, PC/104 add-on cards, PC/104 peripherals, displays, software and enclosures.
