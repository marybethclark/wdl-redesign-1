WDL OFFERS COREMODULE 720 FROM ADLINK

Pittsboro, NC – 28 October 2011 – WDL Systems proudly distributes the CoreModule® 720 PC/104-Plus SBC with Intel® Atom™ Processor E600 Series from Ampro by ADLINK™.

Ampro by ADLINK's CoreModule 720 is based on the Intel Atom Processor E600 series for low power consumption in space constrained, Extreme Rugged environments. A PC/104-Plus Single Board Computer with PCI and ISA bus connectivity, the CoreModule 720 provides an integrated 4GB Solid State Drive, CAN bus, SATA, and a broad range of peripheral I/O support.

CoreModule 720 Features
• Intel® Atom™ Processor E600 series from 600 MHz up to 1.6 GHz
• Up to 2 GB soldered DDR2 SDRAM at 800 MHz
• 24-bit LVDS and SDVO support
• Intel® Platform Controller Hub EG20T for USB, LAN, SDIO, Serial & CAN bus and SATA
• Optional Solid State Drive onboard
• PC/104-Plus (ISA and PCI)
• Extended Temperature: -40°C to +85°C
• 50% Thicker PCB for high vibration environments

For more information and to order Ampro by ADLINK's CoreModule 720 PC/104-Plus, please visit the CoreModule 720 Section of the WDL Systems website: www.wdlsystems.com. E-mail: sales@wdlsystems.com.
