PC/104 Modems from 3JTech NOW AVAILABLE from EMJ

 Rugged Modems Offer Cost-Effective Way to Communicate Over Standard Phone Lines

 Apex, NC – 16 May 2001 - Communicating with a PC/104 stack is easier than ever with PC/104 modems from 3Jtech and WDL Systems. 3JTech 33.6K and 56K PC/104 modems are the most reliable fax/data modems available for industrial PCs.

Typical applications include:

• Utility Systems
• Remote Diagnostics
• Industrial Control
• Meter reading
• Office Systems
• Vending Machines
• Gaming Machines
• Security Systems
• TV Control Systems
• EPOS
• Card readers
• Scanners
• Barcode readers
• TV/Satellite set top box
• Pay Per View

ABOUT 3JTECH
3JTech’s rugged modems offer a cost-effective way to communicate with a PC/104 stack over standard phone lines. 3JTech modems are currently in stock and available for same day shipping from EMJ. Visit the 3JTech website at http://www.3jtech.com


ABOUT WDL Systems
WDL Systems, a division of EMJ America Inc., distributes a full line of single board computers, CompactPCI solutions, Flash solutions, PC Card disk drives, PC Card readers, PC/104 add-on cards, PC/104 peripherals, displays, software and enclosures. EMJ is a publicly traded company with revenues of $150 million and can be found on the Toronto Stock Exchange under the symbol EMJ.
