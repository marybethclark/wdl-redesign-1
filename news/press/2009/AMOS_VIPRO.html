Hello Friends of WDL Systems,
 
WDL Systems is proud to announce A SPECIAL PRICE PROMOTION for two media-rich products from VIA TECHNOLOGIES.

The AMOS-3000 Embedded Box PC features VIA's ultra compact EPIA P700 Pico-ITX form factor board. Constructed with heavy-duty steel, aluminum and copper for superb heat dissipation, the VIA AMOS-3000 is designed to provide an entirely fanless brick computer that can be used for a variety of applications.

The AMOS-3000 is on sale for $295 through July 31, 2009

The VIPRO VP7710 10.4" Touchscreen Panel PC is an embedded panel computer with a 10.4" TFT LCD and resistive touch screen. The VIPRO VP7710 is powered by a 1.6 GHz VIA Eden ULV / 1.0 GHz C7 processor, making it an ideal solution for applications that require low power consumption, fanless and noise-free operation with multiple IO ports.

The VIPRO VP7710 is on sale starting at $729 through July 31, 2009
