Connect Tech's Xtreme I/O Opto is a 48-bit isolated digital input/output board with FPGA technology allowing for flexible integration and customization. Xtreme I/O Opto high performance multi-port serial cards are ideal for embedded technology applications in the military, aerospace, medical and industrial sectors.

Xtreme I/O Opto FEATURES:
• PCI-104 & PC/104-Plus Compliant
• 48 Isolated Digital I/O Channels
• 24 (ea.) optically isolated inputs/outputs
• 3kV isolation on all channels
• FPGA Register Controlled Device
• Interrupt Generation Capability
• +0 to +40V DC Voltage Range
