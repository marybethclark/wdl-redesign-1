PCIe/104
to PCI Express
Adapter

February 2013

PCIe/104 to PCI Express Top Stacking Adapter Boards from Connect Tech extend the capabilities of your PCIe/104 system. This board allows users to install a PCI Express card into a PCIe/104 single board computer system. The board offers compatibility with a wide range of SBCs with a PCI/104-Express (PCIe/104) stack up configuration.

FEATURES:
• Compatible with PCIe/104 and PCI/104-Express
• Supports x1, x4, x8, x16 PCI Express cards
• Custom metal support bracket for system stability
• All PCIe/104 lanes direct connect to PCI Express lanes
• Custom PCI Express metal support bracket
• Dimensions: 3.55” x 3.775”
• Extended Temperature (-40C to 85C)
