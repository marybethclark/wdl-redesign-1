WDL SYSTEMS NOW SHIPPING VDX2-6554 PC/104 SBCs by ICOP TECHNOLOGY

Pittsboro, NC - 6 August 2014 - WDL Systems proudly distributes the VDX2-6554 PC/104 Single Board Computer with Vortex86DX2 SoC from ICOP Technology.

The VDX2-6554 PC/104 SBC is equipped with the DM&P SoC CPU Vortex86DX2 at 800MHz and features up to 1GB DDR2 RAM onboard. Its robust I/O interface includes SATA 7P x1, RS-232/485 x4, USB (2.0) x2, USB hot SWAP x1 (Optional), Parallel x1. 16-bit GPIO x2, and 10/100Mbps Ethernet x1. VDX2-6554 display options include VGA, LCD and LVDS with TFT Flat Panel interface support and resolution up to 1280x1024 with 16M colors.

The VDX2-6554 also boasts a Watchdog Timer that is software programmable from 30.5 µsec. to 512 sec. Overall, the VDX2-6554 PC/104 SBC is a cost-effective, energy efficient option offering extremely high quality and reliability for a wide variety of applications.

VDX2-6554 Features:
            • DM&P SoC CPU Vortex86DX2- 800MHz
            • PC/104 standard compliant
            • 512MB/1GB DDR2 onboard
            • Watchdog Timer, software programmable from 30.5 µsec. to 512 sec.
            • Integrated 2D VGA chip, share system memory 16MB/32MB
            • Integrated ALC 262 HD Audio
            • Integrated 10/100Mbps Ethernet
            • Onboard 4MB SPI Flash Disk
            • Support SATA interface
            • Operation Temperature: -20°C ~ +70°C
