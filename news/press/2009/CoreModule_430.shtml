WDL SYSTEMS NOW SHIPPING COREMODULE 430 from AMPRO by ADLINK

Pittsboro, NC – 11 June 2009 – WDL Systems announces availability of the CoreModule 430 PC/104 Single Board Computer from Ampro by ADLINK Technology. The PC/104 compliant CoreModule 430 SBC features the DM&P SoC (System on Chip) Vortex86SX Microprocessor at 300MHz or 800MHz. With the CoreModule 430, ADLINK continues to support users of the PC/104 standard that Ampro invented in 1992. The CoreModule 430 will drop directly into applications that use EOL CPUs, allowing system OEMs to continue to leverage their investments in enclosures, cables, software and certifications. For new designs, CoreModule 430 is also capable of proven PC/104 I/O support.

“The CoreModule 430 is a robust and flexible PC/104 CPU solution for low power systems,” says WDL Systems technologist, Donnie Springfield. “The 300MHz Vortex86SX-based CoreModule 430 provides a solid computing platform, and the 800MHz Vortex86DX version is a pin-compatible drop-in alternative for more demanding applications.”

The CoreModule 430 is integrated with the following features:

• 300MHz Vortex86SX or 800MHz Vortex86DX
• Ultra Low Power
• PC/104 Expansion, 486 Migration
• (4) Serial ports, (2) are RS232/422/485
• (2) USB 2.0 ports, (8) GPIO pins
• PS/2 Keyboard and Mouse ports
• 10/100BaseT Ethernet, 3D Video
• EIDE - Enhanced DMA 100 Synchronous IDE interface supports up to (2) hard drives
• Compact Flash Socket on primary IDE
• DRAM 256MB soldered DDR2
• Supports 3.3V, 5V and TFT Flat Panels
• -40° to +85°C extended temperature option
