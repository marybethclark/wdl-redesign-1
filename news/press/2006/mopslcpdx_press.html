WDL SYSTEMS IS PLEASED TO ANNOUNCE MOPSlcdLX PC/104-Plus BOARD IS NOW SHIPPING

Pittsboro, NC – 15 August 2006 – WDL Systems is pleased to announce that the MOPSlcdLX, a low power PC/104-Plus Single Board Computer from Kontron, is now shipping.

The MOPSlcdLX is a highly integrated PC104-Plus board featuring a 500MHz AMD LX800™ processor and extremely low power consumption. It includes onchip Graphics VRAM for high speed LCD/CRT, ISA and PCI IO card expansion, and is ROHS compliant. The MOPSlcdLX is Plug&Work compatible with all Kontron MOPS PC/104 products.

"This board provides a significant performance increase with extremely low power consumption," Mark Estill, president of WDL said. "Engineers will be pleasantly surprised when they use this board for their RoHS applications."

For more information and to order the MOPSlcdLX from WDL Systems online, please visit our website's MOPSlcdLX PC/104-plus section.

WDL Systems (formerly EMJ Embedded Systems) is the Embedded Products Source. WDL Systems distributes the latest technology to provide customers with Commercial-Off-The-Shelf (COTS) products for all embedded requirements.

WDL Systems' PC/104 products include Kontron's MOPSlcd Single Board Computers, Lippert's Cool LiteRunner, the MZ104 from Tri-M, PC/104 Analog and Digital I/O Boards from Acces IO, Connect Tech PC/104 and PC/104 Opto Isolated Multiport Serial Boards, and Eagle's PC/104 A/D Boards and Digital I/O & Analog Input Boards.

PC/104-Plus products currently distributed by WDL Systems include Eagle's PC/104-plus A/D Boards, Connect Tech's PC/104-Plus Multiport Serial Boards, Kontron's MOPSlcdLX, speedMOPSlcdCE, and speedMOPSlcdPM, Lippert's Cool FrontRunner, Cool RoadRunner III and Cool RoadRunner IV, and Tri-M's MZ104plus.

In addition to PC/104 and PC/104-Plus products, WDL Systems distributes Flash solutions, PC Card Disk Drives, PC Card Readers, PC/104 Add-on Cards, PC/104 Peripherals, Alphanumeric Displays, Embedded PC's, Embedded Modem solutions, Embedded Software, and PC/104 Enclosures.
