WDL Systems Now Shipping Connect Tech’s Xtreme/GbE Managed Carrier Ethernet Switch

Pittsboro, NC - 29 July 2015 - WDL Systems is now shipping Connect Tech’s Xtreme/GbE Managed Carrier Ethernet Switch for carrier grade ethernet switching capabilities in an extremely small embedded form factor.

Excellent for high-end applications such as Mobile and Microwave backhaul, the Xtreme/GbE Managed Carrier Ethernet Switch is also a highly reliable way to communicate with 10/100/1000 devices in an embedded system. Xtreme/GbE is powered by the latest generation Carrier Ethernet Switch engine the Vitesse 7428/7429 with embedded 416MHz MIPS 32-bit CPU, 1GB DDR2 external memory and DMA-based frame extraction and insertion support timing over packet, Ethernet OAM, and performance monitoring.

Available in models with RJ-45 or Rugged Positive Locking Headers, Connect Tech’s Xtreme/GbE Managed Carrier Ethernet Switch is an embedded Ethernet switch ideal for any type of environment.

Xtreme/GbE Managed Carrier Ethernet Switch Features:

  • Conduction cooled Heatplate or Air cooled Heatsink option
  • 8 or 12 Port 10/100/1000 Mbps Switch
  • Web GUI or CLI Management
  • Carrier Grade Ethernet Switching
  • Available with RJ-45 or Rugged Locking connectors
  • PC/104 Compliant: 4.550" x 4.393" or smaller
  • Extended Temperature Range -40°C to +85°C
