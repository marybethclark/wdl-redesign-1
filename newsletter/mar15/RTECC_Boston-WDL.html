RTECC is the premiere embedded event for in-depth technical training and discussions on future design strategies and market opportunities. Celebrating its 28th year, RTECC continues to provide a free event with minimal marketing fluff that focuses on connecting chip, board and system designs with the embedded software community. The event offers a broad education on the latest design strategies, methodologies, applications and technologies for capitalizing on current and future market opportunities.Taking place on April 8th at the Sheraton Framingham outside Boston, this event will combine technical presentations and hands-on labs with demonstrations and exhibits to provide a comprehensive tutorial on building optimized hardware and software products for the embedded marketplace, as well as forging new opportunities in the burgeoning markets of IoT and Cloud Computing.
