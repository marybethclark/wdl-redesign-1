WDL Systems Offers PerfecTron's SR Series of Fanless Rugged Systems

Pittsboro, NC - 25 August 2015 - WDL Systems is a proud distributor of PerfecTron’s Stack Rack (SR) series of rugged fanless systems – SR100, SR200, and SR700.

The SR series features the Intel® Core™ i7-4700EQ Haswell Processor and Intel® QM87 MIL-STD Platform Controller Hub (PCH) that can be operated at full speed over a wide temperature range of -40°C to +70°C. The SR100 can be optionally integrated with the PerfecTron storage extension module SK401. The SR200 supports 4 independent display ports that can be combined with the PerfecTron NVIDIA graphic card module  SK210. The SR700 features M12 type connectors and carries an IP65 rating for protection against dirt, dust and water, and provides shock and vibration immunity exceeding MIL-STD 810G Compliance.  

PerfecTron’s SR series of fanless rugged systems are designed to meet the strict standards of MIL-STD 810G to withstand harsh environments. Each MIL-STD 810G certified unit undergoes testing procedures for resistance to shock, vibration, dust, humidity, and extreme temperatures.

PerfecTron’s Stack Rack (SR) series of rugged fanless systems are perfect portable solutions for the fields of military and defense, transportation, factory automation and digital signage applications, as well as mining and offshore drilling.

SR100 / SR200 Features:

  • Intel® Core™ i7 Haswell, BGA type Core i7-4700EQ, Intel® QM87 PCH
  • MIL-STD 810G Compliance
  • XR-DIMM up to 8 GB RAM
  • OnBoard uSSD SATA III up to 64 GB
  • Multi-Displays (SR100 x2 DP, SR200 x4 DP with NVIDIA GT730M)
  • 2 x mPCIe Expansion slot
  • 2 x PCIe Intel® GbE, 4 x USB 3.0, 1 x COM ports
  • 9V~36V DC-in with power delay on/off
  • Extended Temperature -40 °C to 70 °C

SR 700 Features:

  • Intel® Core™ i7 Haswell, BGA type Core i7-4700EQ, Intel® QM87 PCH
  • Fully IP65 classified
  • MIL-STD 810G Compliance
  • XR-DIMM up to 8 GB RAM
  • OnBoard uSSD SATA III up to 64 GB
  • Displays by 1 x VGA (M12 type)
  • 2 x PCIe Intel® Gigabit Ethernet (M12 type)
  • 2 x USB 2.0 (M12 type), 1 x COM ports (M12 type)
  • Extended Temperature -40 to 70 °C
