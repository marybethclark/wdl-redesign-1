WDL Systems presents MultiConnect® Conduit™ Starter Kit for LoRa® Technology by MultiTech

Pittsboro, NC - 19 August 2016 - WDL Systems proudly presents MultiTech's MultiConnect® Conduit™ Starter Kit for LoRa® Technology - a single box, pre-configured solution providing all necessary components to build and connect a LoRa IoT proof of concept (PoC) to the cloud.  Utilizing the Conduit Starter Kit customers can now easily leverage the power within the MultiConnect Conduit to speed deployment and significantly reduce the time to market for new LoRa ideas and products.

All major components of the Conduit Starter Kit are pre-configured for use with the Conduit platform and select MultiTech cloud platforms and only require simple actions to finish set-up.  Pre-configured MultiConnect Conduit, MultiTech’s intelligent and programmable IoT gateway, MultiConnect® mDot™, a LoRa® Alliance certified, LoRaWAN™ RF module and cloud connectors simplify and quicken PoC or site survey execution.

The Conduit Starter Kits are available in 868MHz and 915MHz versions designed for operation in the European Union and North America market places, respectively.
