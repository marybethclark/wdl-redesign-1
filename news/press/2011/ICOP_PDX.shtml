WDL SYSTEMS SUPPLIES ICOP VORTEX86MX+ INDUSTRIAL PANEL PCS WITH Vortex86MX+

Pittsboro, NC – 6 December 2011 – WDL Systems is proud to distribute Industrial Panel PCs with Vortex86MX+ (PMX) SoC onboard from ICOP Technology Inc.

The PMX-089T has a 8.9" 16:9 touchscreen display, and the PMX-057T has a 5.7" 4:3 touchscreen. Both compact Industrial Panel PCs feature the new Vortex86MX+ 1GHz low power SoC, DDR2 memory, integrated 10/100 LAN, 2xUSB 2.0 ports and an RS-232 serial port. PMX-089T and PMX-057T are compatible with DOS, Linux, Windows Embedded Compact and Windows XP environments. PMX series Panel PCs from ICOP Technology are the perfect choice for HMI, interactive Digital Signage, Point of Sale and Kiosk applications with modest budgets.

Features of the PMX-089T and PMX-057T :
• TFT LCD with resistive touchscreen
• DM&P Vortex86MX+ 1GHz onboard
• 1GB DDR2 system memory onboard
• HD Audio
• Compact Flash and microSD slots
• Wi-Fi Optional
• Fanless and low power consumption
• Metal chassis with compact design

For more information and to order the PMX-089T and PMX-057T, please visit the ICOP Industrial Panel PC with Vortex86MX+ section of the WDL Systems website: www.wdlsystems.com. E-mail: sales@wdlsystems.com.
