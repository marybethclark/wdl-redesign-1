WDL SYSTEMS IS NOW SHIPPING THE EE25.2 SERIES HARD DRIVES FROM SEAGATE

Pittsboro, NC – 30 September 2007 – WDL Systems is now shipping the EE25.2 Series Ruggedized Hard Drives from Seagate. The Seagate EE25.2 Series 2.5-inch 30-, 40-, 60- and 80-Gbyte disc drives feature SATA and PATA interface and are designed for extreme environments, such as automotive, industrial PC, field computing and military applications. RunOn Technology reliably delivers multimedia content under high-vibration, high-humidity and extreme temperature conditions. High storage capacities bring worry-free media and GPS services to the automobile, capacity-hungry imaging and data applications to critical field applications.

“WDL Systems is proud to distribute the new EE25.2 Series Ruggedized Hard Drives from Seagate,” says WDL Systems president, Mark Estill. “All the EE25 Hard Drives are cost-effective and offer large storage capacities when highly reliable storage is necessary for extreme environmental conditions.”

For more information and to order EE25.1 Series Hard Drives from WDL Systems, please visit our website's EE25.2 page.

Seagate EE25.2 Features Include:
- 30-, 40-, 60- and 80-Gbyte capacities
- Extreme and Rugged operating temperature ranges (–30ºC to 85ºC and –20ºC to 75ºC)
- High-humidity and high-altitude operation
- Up to 2.0 Gs operating vibration tolerance
- Long-duration shock of 110 Gs
- PATA and SATA Interface

Seagate Technology has been at the forefront of the storage industry for more than 25 years. No other hard drive manufacturer matches Seagate’s breadth of solutions. Seagate delivers advanced solutions for every industry that requires digital storage. The company leads the industry in research and development, with a focus on bringing to market new technologies. Seagate hard drives enable mission-critical storage systems to run cooler, faster and more reliably, helping businesses reduce costs and improve efficiency.
