WDL SYSTEMS DISTRIBUTES SEALEVEL PRODUCTS

Pittsboro, NC – 25 October 2010 – WDL Systems is proud to distribute embedded products from Sealevel Systems, Inc. Sealevel's commitment to providing the high quality, reliable I/O solutions is reflected in a Lifetime Warranty that is standard on all Sealevel manufactured products.

Sealevel's 3551 (SIO-104) PC/104 Serial Interface board provides one RS-232 port capable of data rates to 460.8K bps. The board implements all modem control signals for maximum compatibility with a wide range of peripherals. The 3551 utilizes a 16C850 UART to provide 128-byte Tx/Rx FIFOs for maximum reliability in data intensive applications. Ordering options allow the choice of UARTs for custom baud rates and 9-bit protocols. Sealevel's SeaCOM software utilities make installation and operation easy.

Sealevel 3551 (SIO-104) Features Include:
• PC/104 Host Interface
• RS-232 Port, 850 UART
• Max Data Rate 460.8K bps
• Max Data Distance 50 feet
• Operating Temperature 0 - +70C

For more information and to order Sealevel's 3551 (SIO-104) PC/104 Serial Interface board, please visit its page in Serial Communications or the Vendor Page on the WDL Systems website: www.wdlsystems.com. Email: sales@wdlsystems.com.
