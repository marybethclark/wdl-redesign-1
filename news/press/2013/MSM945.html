WDL SYSTEMS OFFERS MICROSPACE® MSM945 from KONTRON

Pittsboro, NC – 1 March 2013 – WDL Systems proudly distributes Kontron's MICROSPACE® MSM945/P PCI/104-Express Single Board Computers with Intel® Core™2 Duo for building reliable embedded PCs for military and aerospace applications.

The MICROSPACE MSM945/P SBC has all of the standard PC interfaces plus ethernet LAN, optional DVI and LVDS (dual screen) and a sound controller. Equipped with the CPU Intel® Core™2 Duo L7400 in the SMX945 with COM Express, this product has the highest performance on the PC/104 form factor. The optional PCI/104-Express bus (PCI & PCI Express) and 6 USB interfaces are available as functional extensions.

MICROSPACE® MSM945 Features:
 • Video streaming for HD videos
• Dual screen applications
• Image processing, video monitoring
• Calculation-intensive data processing
