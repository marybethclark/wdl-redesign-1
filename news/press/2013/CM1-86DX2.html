WDL SYSTEMS PROVIDES ADLINK'S COREMODULE1-86DX2 PC/104 SBC

Pittsboro, NC - 23 September 2013 - WDL Systems proudly distributes a powerful yet cost-effective PC/104 solution with the CoreModule1-86DX2 PC/104 Single Board Computer from ADLINK.

Giving a performance boost to legacy PC/104 systems, ADLINK’s CoreModule1-86DX2 integrates a powerful yet efficient DMP Vortex86DX2 SoC with graphic and audio controllers, plus an additional GBit Ethernet controller to form a complete PC with all the standard peripherals already onboard. The CM1-86DX2 supports Smart Embedded Management Agent (SEMA) functions, including watchdog timer, temperature monitoring, and fail-safe BIOS support, further insuring the system’s operational reliability.

CoreModule® CM1-86DX2 Features:
• Ultra low power, DM&P Vortex86DX2 SoC
• Full ISA bus support with built in redundancy
• 512MB/1G onboard DDR2 memory
• 2x Ethernet ports
• 8x GPIO
• VGA and 18/24-bit single channel LVDS
• HD Audio for CM1-86DX2-1G version
• PCI Express Mini Card and optional MicroSD slot
• Extreme temperature range: -40°C to +85°C
