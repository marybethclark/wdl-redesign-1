WDL Systems offers the embedded marketplace a comprehensive selection of embedded products and services.

At WDL Systems we actively seek the latest in embedded technology in order to provide our customers with the most up-to-date and far-reaching solutions. Developers calling WDL Systems have access to a comprehensive selection of embedded products, a knowledgeable sales staff, technical support, and assistance with configuration and integration issues.

In business since 1990, WDL Systems (formerly EMJ Embedded Systems) is an integral player in embedded systems distribution. As an inactive member of the PC/104 Consortium, we continue to support standards that make embedded development easier and less risky.
