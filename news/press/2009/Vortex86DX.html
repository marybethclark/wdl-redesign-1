WDL SYSTEMS DISTRIBUTING VORTEX86DX PC/104 SBC FROM ICOP

Pittsboro, NC – 13 April 2009 – WDL Systems is now distributing Vortex86DX PC/104 Single Board Computers from ICOP Technology. The VDX-6354 features the DM&P x86 SoC (System on Chip) Vortex86DX Microprocessor at 800MHz. The PC/104 compliant VDX-6354 is designed for ultra low power consumption (less than 1 watt). It comes with optional PC/104+ expansion, various I/O (RS-232, Parallel, USB and GPIO), Watchdog Timer, onboard 32MB VGA Memory, Integrated 10/100Mbps Ethernet, Power Management, MTBF counter, 256MB DDR2, and Audio out.

“The VDX-6354 is an outstanding addition to WDL Systems’ extensive line of PC/104 Single Board Computers,” remarks WDL Systems technologist, Donnie Springfield. “The small footprint, low-power and low-cost design of the VDX-6354 make it an attractive and efficient solution for a multitude of applications.”

On-board features of the VDX-6354 include soldered 256MB DDR2 system memory, XGI Volari Z9s Chipset VGA and TFT Flat Panel Interface Support with onboard 32MB VGA Memory (resolution up to 1280 x 1024, 16M colors), Integrated 10/100Mbps Ethernet and Onboard 4MB SPI Flash Disk. The VDX-6354 offers extended operating temperatures (-20°C ~ +70°C and optional -40°C ~ +85°C) and PC/104+ optional capability.

VDX-6354 Features Include:
• DM&P SoC CPU Vortex86DX- 800MHz
• PC/104 standard compliant, PC/104+ Optional
• 256MB DDR2 onboard
• Watchdog Timer, software programmable from 30.5 µsec. to 512 sec.
• XGI Volari Z9s Chipset, onboard 32MB VGA Memory
• Audio out
• Integrated 10/100Mbps Ethernet
• Onboard 4MB SPI Flash Disk
• Support MSTI EmbedDisk Module
• 44-pin IDE to microSD (Optional)
• Operation Temperature: -20°C ~ +70°C, -40°C ~ +85°C (Optional)
