WDL SYSTEMS ADDS MILITARY SPECIFICATION INNODISK FLASH TO LINECARD

Pittsboro, NC – 5 January 2010 – WDL Systems announces the addition of military specification (Mil-Spec) flash to its product offerings. The InnoRobust Drive Series of Embedded Flash Storage from InnoDisk includes 2.5" SATA SSD and SSD Ultra and 2.5" PATA SSD (solid state disk). These drives are appropriate for Military Specifications. The InnoRobust SSD not only provides high reliability performance, but also introduces data erasing features targeting military, defense, and aerospace applications. The shock, vibration, and altitude analysis were performed in accordance with the MIL-STD standards. Features include: QEraser, SEraser, Write Protect; Operating Temp: 40C ~ +85C; Altitude: 80,000 Ft.; MTBF: 4 million hours.

InnoRobust Ultra SATA, SATA, and PATA Features Include:

• Data Security/Protection: Qeraser/Seraser/Write Protect/Destroy
• Conformal Coating: Preventing corrosion/MIL-I-46058C silicon conformal coating
• High Reliability:
• Altitude: 80,000 ft
• Wide Temp: -40"C to 85"C
• Thermal shock : MIL-STD-810F 503.4 Procedure II
• Drop : MIL-STD-810F 516.5 Procedure IV
• Transit Drop specification
• Shock : 1500G Shock/
• MIL-STD-810F 516.5 specification
• 40g/11ms/18 shocks (3 shocks for +- X,YZ axis)
• Vibration : 20G Vibration / MIL-STD-810F 514.5C-1 specification
• Thermal shock: MIL-STD-810-503.4 Procedure II
• (-40"C to 85"C, 10 cycles)
• Humidity: 5%~95%
