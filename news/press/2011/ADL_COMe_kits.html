COM EXPRESS™ STARTER KITS by ADLINK AVAILABLE TO SHIP TODAY!

Pittsboro, NC – 1 July 2011 – WDL Systems offers a wide variety of ADLINK COM Express™ Starter Kits to help designers get started in minutes with ADLINK COM Express boards. ADLINK COM Express Starter Kits from WDL Systems are in stock and available to ship today!

ADLINK COM Express Starter Kits consists of a COM Express™ core module with an ATX form factor reference carrier board that offers one PCI Express® x16 graphics slot, five PCI Express x1 slots, two PCI slots, serial ATA, SDVO, CRT, LVDS, TV-out, USB 2,0, Gigabit LAN, and Super I/O. The Starter Kit comes with full baseboard schematics, COM Express design guides, product manuals and BSP. Everything needed to get a user going with his own baseboard design and software verification in no time.

COM Express Starter Kit Features Include:
• COM Express™ Module
• CPU, Memory
• Express-BASE Reference Carrier Board
• Thermal Solution (heatspreader and heatsink)
• Schematics, Design Guide, and User Manuals
• ADLINK USB stick with Documentation, Drivers, BSPs, Libraries

WDL Systems' online catalog for ADLINK COM Express Starter Kits includes :

• ETX-AT N270 Starter Kit
• ETX-PVR Starter Kit
• Express-AT Starter Kit
• Express-ATC Starter Kit
• Express-CB-i7 Starter Kit
• Express-LPC Starter Kit

For more information and to order other ADLINK COM Express modules, kits and accessories visit the Computer On Modules section on the WDL Systems website: www.wdlsystems.com. Email: sales@wdlsystems.com.
