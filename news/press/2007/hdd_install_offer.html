FREE HDD INSTALLATION OFFER FROM WDL SYSTEMS -- with Purchase of EE25 Hard Drive and Qualifying eBox

Pittsboro, NC – 22 October 2007 – WDL Systems is now offering FREE installation service with purchase of EE25 Series 2.5" ruggedized hard drives and qualifying eBox models. The EE25 Series 2.5" Hard Drives for Extreme Environments by Seagate are ideal for use inside Embedded PCs such as the eBox from ICOP. The EE25.1 and EE25.2 Hard Drives, ranging in capacity from 20GB to 80GB, are designed for rugged applications such as automotive, industrial PC, field computing and military applications. The eBox from ICOP is a low-power, fanless Embedded PC offered by WDL Systems. The following eBoxes support a 2.5” hard drive upgrade: eBox-2300SX-H, eBox-4850, eBox-4851, eBox-4853, eBox-3850, eBox-3851, and eBox-3853.

“WDL Systems is pleased to offer free installations of EE25 Series Ruggedized Hard Drives from Seagate with the purchase an upgradeable eBox from ICOP,” says WDL Systems president, Mark Estill. “Without a doubt, the eBox is revolutionizing the Embedded PC market. EE25 Hard Drives from Seagate are a perfect cost-effective solution for highly reliable storage in extreme environments.”

For more information and to order EE25 Series Hard Drives and eBoxes from WDL Systems, please visit our Free HDD Installation Promotion Page.

Promotion Details: Hard drive and eBox must be purchased directly from WDL Systems to qualify for free installation offer. Offer does not include software installation. eBox and EE25 HDD must be purchased at same time from WDL Systems.

EE25 Series Features Include:
- 20-, 30-, 40-, 60-, 80- Gbyte capacities
- Rugged operating temperature range (-20º to 75ºC)
- Extreme operating temperature range (–30º to 85ºC)
- High-humidity and high-altitude operation
- Up to 2.0 Gs operating vibration tolerance
- Long-duration shock of 110 Gs
- PATA and SATA Interface

eBOX Features Include:
- 10/100 Ethernet
- Linux, Windows CE and XPe compatible
- 44-pin IDE connector
- Fanless
- Low Power (20W max)
- 1 year warranty
