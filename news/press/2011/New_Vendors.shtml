WDL SYSTEMS NEW VENDORS: ARBOR, Artila, DIT, LongWin, Sealevel, SECO

Pittsboro, NC – 3 January, 2011 – WDL Systems rings in the New Year with six new vendors whose product offerings range from a wide variety of embedded systems products such as: Medical PCs, Embedded Linux ARM9 Industrial PCs, Industrial Keyboards, PC/104 Digital I/O Modules, Asynchronous Serial Boards, and QSeven™ (Q7) modules.

ARBOR Embedded & Networking Computing:
Arbor's products have been successfully applied into the fileds of transportation, e-Service, medical, manufacturing automation, telecommunication, networking, information system and military.
WDL Systems is proud to distribute ARBOR Medical PCs:
  • ARBOR Medical PC
    • Fanless Intel Atom 1.6GHz Medical PC
    • Gladius Portable Medical PC (Intel Atom)
    • Portable Tablet PC for Mobile Clinical Assistant
    • Panel PC for Mobile Medical Station (Core 2 Duo)

Artila Electronics focuses on developing easily-accessible, flexibly-programmed industrial ARM-based embedded Linux solutions, including embedded ARM9 Linux single board computers, box computers, system-on-modules, and serial-to-Ethernet embedded modules.
WDL Systems is proud to distribute Artila products:

  • Artila Embedded PC
    • Industrial PAC
    • Matrix Embedded Linux ARM9 Industrial PC
    • Matrix Windows CE ARM9 Industrial PC
  • Artila Serial Communications and PC/104 Peripherals
    • Aport Serial-to-Ethernet Gateway Server
    • Serial-to-Ethernet Embedded Module
  • Artila Single Board Computers
    • Matrix Embedded Linux ARM9 SBC


DIT (Display Integration Technologies) specializes in the design and manufacturing of High Definition Broadcast Quality LCD Products. These products include Field, Studio, Rack, and Custom Monitor Solutions with integrated analog, digital and HD/SD-SDI inputs.
WDL Systems is proud to distribute DIT Industrial Keyboards:

  • Industrial Keyboards
    • RD1U Rack Drawer Keyboard/Monitor/Mouse
    • RDKB Rack Drawer Keyboard


LongWin Computer Inc. is an electronic designing and consulting company founded in 1994, that designs, develops and consults various electronic products from consumer to industrial field.
WDL Systems is proud to distribute LongWin products:

  • Data Acquisition
    • PC/104 Digital I/O Modules
  • Power Solutions
    • 90W DC-DC Power Supply Modules


Sealevel Systems, Inc. has provided innovative, reliable hardware and software products to enable computer connectivity and control for over 20 years. Sealevel manufactures more than 300 standard I/O products with ISO 9001: 2000 registration and a standard lifetime warranty.
WDL Systems is proud to distribute Sealevel products:

  • Serial Communications
    • Compact PCI Asynchronous Serial Boards
    • ISA Asynchronous Serial Boards
    • LP PCI Asynchronous Serial Boards
    • PC/104 Asynchronous Serial Boards
    • PCI Asynchronous Serial Boards
    • USB Asynchronous Serial Adapters


SECO s.r.l. is a founding member of the QSeven™ Consortium. Since 1979, SECO has been a highly creative influence in the embedded electronics industry. Qseven™ modules have a standardized form factor of 70mm x 70mm with specified pinouts based on the high speed MXM system standardized connector, and are mounted onto an application specific carrier board.
WDL Systems is proud to distribute SECO products:

  • Computer On Modules
    • QSeven™ Carrier Boards
    • QSeven™ Development Kit
    • QSeven™ Modules - Quadmo747
