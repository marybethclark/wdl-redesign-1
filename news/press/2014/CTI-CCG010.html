WDL SYSTEMS OFFERS COM EXPRESS® TYPE 10 CARRIER BOARDS by CTI

Pittsboro, NC - 1 October 2014 - WDL Systems proudly distributes Connect Tech's CCG010 COM Express® Type 10 Mini Carrier Board for space constrained applications, harsh environments and demanding conditions. The CCG010 COM Express® Type 10 Mini Carrier Board features rugged, locking connectors and supports extended temperature ranges of -40°C to +85°C for ultimate durability.
 
CCG010 COM Express® Type 10 Mini Carrier Board Features Include:
• Extremely small size, 84mm x 55mm
• All ruggedized latching connectors
• 2x mPCIe, mSATA, SATA, 2x GbE, 6x USB, 2x RS-232/422/485
• LVDS, DisplayPort/HDMI/DVI/VGA, HD Audio
• Support for the latest generation of low powered CPU's
• Extended Temperature Range
• Lifetime Warranty and Free technical support

Connect Tech's CCG010 COM Express® Type 10 Mini Carrier Board received the Editor's Choice Award for Military Embedded Systems June 2014 issue. Connect Tech's engineers developed the CCG010 COM Express Type 10 Mini Carrier Board for rugged small form factor (SFF) applications in unmanned systems, avionics, military ground vehicles, digital mapping, and others with high performance processing needs constrained Size, Weight, and Power (SWaP) environments. The CCG010 is the smallest of the Connect Tech CCGxxx line of COM Express Carrier Boards.
