KONTRON NAMES WDL SYSTEMS DISTRIBUTOR OF THE YEAR FOR 2005

Pittsboro, NC – 30 May 2006 – Kontron has named WDL Systems Distributor of the Year from among its 30 worldwide distributors. The announcement was made on May 22 in Austria, during Kontron’s annual distributors’ conference. WDL Systems was cited for achieving top sales volume in 2005.

This is the fourth time in five years that WDL (formerly EMJ Embedded Systems) was the number one overall distributor for Kontron.

WDL Systems President Mark Estill was present to accept the Distributor of the Year Award and said, "I am very pleased to accept this recognition on behalf of WDL Systems for the fourth time. Kontron has been an excellent partner, and we would not be receiving this award without their support. Kontron’s products continue to be cutting-edge. The features and flexibility of these products put them in high demand in the American market."

“WDL Systems has been a valued partner of Kontron from many years, and we look forward to increasing that relationship as we move forward,” says Mike Kadri, VP of Sales for Kontron America. "WDL provides consistent value and competent, professional service to our mutual customers. We can count on WDL to take care of business," adds Fred Barden, Director of Embedded Sales at Kontron America.

WDL Systems is a stocking distributor for Kontron products. Its website www.wdlsystems.com provides spec sheets and online ordering and technical support. Sales and technical support are also available by calling WDL Sytems toll-free. WDL also promotes Kontron products through its active participation in embedded industry trade shows and advertising.


Kontron is a leading global embedded computer technology company. Kontron supplies a diversified customer base of OEMs, system integrators and application providers in the communications, automation, transportation, medical, military, aerospace, and test and measurement markets. The company helps its customers to considerably reduce their time-to-market and to gain a competitive advantage with products including high-performance open computer platforms and systems, single-board computers, man-machine interfaces and mobile computers. Kontron employs over 1100 people worldwide and has manufacturing facilities in Europe, North America and Asia Pacific. The company is listed on the German TecDAX 30 stock exchange under the symbol "KBC".

For additional information on Kontron, please visit: www.kontron.com

WDL Systems is a privately owned company that distributes a full line of single board computers, Flash solutions, PC Card disk drives, PC Card readers, PC/104 add-on cards, PC/104 peripherals, alphanumeric displays, embedded software, and PC/104 enclosures. Other product specialties include multi-platform internetworking software and Internet security software. WDL Systems distributes the latest technology to provide customers with Commercial-Off-The-Shelf (COTS) products for all embedded requirements.
